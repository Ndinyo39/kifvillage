About Us

KIFARANSA VILLAGE

The KIFARANSA VILLAGE Was founded in 2020 by Mr. Sammy SIMIYU and co- founders devoted to promote French language, encourage reciprocal communication between all levels of learning it and reuniting the french community and those who have intrest and passion to learn with benefits that Come with learning french.
The Kifaransa Village was incorporated in 2022 and, after established opérations in Nairobi in patnership with Alliance Française , it extended its opérations to Coastal and Western/lake region adopting regional strategy under the regional exécutive officials in the other regions.

--Interior design Package
--Building House
--Repairing of Residential Roof
--Renovation of Commercial Office

Our Services

Membership Programs

Mentorship Development Program focuses on self and team development. The program can be customized to suit your company/institution/individual needs in line with your stated objectives.

Team Building

We facilitate team building workshops to create positive relationships and synergy within french teaching schools,organizations/institutions.

Translation and interpretation

Kifaransa village offers French Translation Services with its team of experienced translators all across specialized in diverse fields.

Customized Call Centre

Offer customer Care services in French organisations , a first point of contact for calls from our English speaking customers and the primary point of contact for French speaking customers.

French Tutoring

Compassionate and dedicated french and teachers ready to deliver fully and quality knowledge to the student. Well versed in computer literacy hence making even the online teaching easy.
